About Us

We are a non-profit organisation of volunteers that try to keep our cities and planet clean and greener.
We organise few types of activites in different areas.

We are so proud of our volonteeres that contributed in: planting over 20,000 trees, picking over 30 tons of litter from the streets and making our parks burst in different colours this spring .

Our activities take place every week, and the best thing is that everyone can get iinvolved and contribute to greener future. All you need is to follow the steps bellow, or navigate to our Get Involved section.
